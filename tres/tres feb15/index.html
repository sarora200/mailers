Alysa Craig

Managing Director, M&A
Head of SPAC Practice

Stifel Financial Corp.

Kevin Charlton

CEO

NewHold Investment Corp. (NASDAQ: NHIC)

Alysa has over 20 years of investment banking experience and a track record of executing more than $40 billion of SPAC transactions across multiple industries and geographies, including M&A, IPO and PIPEs. She has followed the SPAC product for over a decade through two different generations of SPACs. She has previously worked at Deutsche Bank, Rothschild, Credit Suisse and Tegris. She holds an MBA degree from The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, with concentration in Finance and Accounting.

Kevin Charlton is the CEO of NewHold Investment Corp (NASDAQ: NHIC) a $172.5mm SPAC focused on industrial technology. He was also President and COO of three prior SPACs: HCAC I, which merged with Blue Bird Bus Company in 2015, HCAC II, which merged with Daseke in 2017, and HCAC III, which merged with NRCG in 2018. In addition to NHIC, he is Co-Chairman of NewHold Enterprises, which focuses on private investments in high-growth industrial subsectors. In addition to NHIC, he is currently on the Boards of American AllWaste, F&S Tools, Macro Energy, and Spirit Realty Capital. Before his career in private equity, Kevin worked with McKinsey and NASA.

Don Duffy

President

ICR

Giovanni Caruso

Partner

Loeb & Loeb LLP

A leading expert on IPOs, SPACs and other complex corporate transactions, Don has led teams in advising hundreds of companies on IPOs, mergers, acquisitions and other complex corporate transactions. Prior to ICR, Don spent over a decade as a portfolio manager and research analyst specializing in public and private investments in the consumer and technology industries. Prior to joining ICR, Don co-founded the asset management firm Meyer, Duffy & Associates and Meyer Duffy Ventures. Don also served on multiple public boards, and has chaired audit, compensation and special committees. He received his B.A. in Finance from St. John’s University.

Giovanni represents both public and private companies in a broad range of corporate and securities matters, including general corporate governance, private placements, mergers and acquisitions and federal law compliance in connection to the Securities Act and Exchange Act and Sarbanes-Oxley, as well as the various exchanges (NASDAQ, NYSE and the NYSE Amex). He also regularly represents issuers and underwriters in SPAC offerings. He has drafted and negotiated a variety of agreements in connection with his representation of clients, and has experience in drafting various securities documents such as annual, quarterly and current reports, registration statements and private placement documents.
