VIP LP Allocator Series:
Family Offices as
Fund & Co-Investors

May 11th, 2021

5:00 PM - 6:30 PM ET

Led by the rise in global wealth, Family Offices have continued to grow and expand their investment coverage and this trend is expected to continue in 2021. Through the disruption brought on by COVID-19, Family Offices have likely faced a rebalancing in their portfolios though continue to focus on long-term goals of preserving wealth while managing risk. The trend toward direct investments is also well documented,though finding quality managers and establishing a solid co-investment program remains essential.


Join us for the first session in the VIP LP Allocator series where our panel of experts answer significant questions.

AGENDA

How are Family Offices managing their succession planning issues and mitigating the
operational, liquidity, and portfolio risk induced by COVID-19?

What investment strategies and geographies are Family Offices targeting to preserve
wealth and diversify their portfolios

What factors contribute to the increasing keenness towards co-investments?

With the growing need for transparency and expectation of better real time data to
drive decision-making, how are Family Offices optimizing technology and
data analytics tools?

As talent becomes increasingly harder to find and compensate, how are Family offices
approaching hiring and resource management?

How are focus areas such as sustainability, ESG, and impact investing shaping the
way Family Offices look at allocating?
