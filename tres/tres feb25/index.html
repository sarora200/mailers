Webinar On SPACs


Our panel of experts analyzed SPACs thoroughly
& this is what they had to say.
 
 

Kevin Charlton, Don Duffy, Alysa Craig & Giovanni Caruso have been working with SPACs for decades and have experienced the beginning of the SPAC movement & its evolution. They answered questions on why this movement has become the biggest attraction for global sponsors & investors.
 
 
 

Key Insights
 
 

SPACs (Special Purpose Acquisition Company) have become a legitimate IPO (Initial Public Offering) alternative and the growing adoption of SPACs will continue to affect the traditional IPO model
 
 

For early-stage companies, the SPAC route provides more certainty earlier on and helps minimize the risks around proceeds, pricing, timing, and execution

 
 

While sponsors were previously reluctant to go the SPAC route, almost every sponsor today would likely be evaluating this as a viable alternative for their portfolio
 
 

The SPAC process with the SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission) has become very streamlined and the rise in institutional adoption is also likely driven in part by the flexibility in reporting allowed by the SEC
 
 

The key to success will be in finding quality targets and closing deals in a timely manner, and teams with prior experience in SPACs are likely to have an advantage in this process
